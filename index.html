What is Bait & Switch Pricing?

Learn how car dealerships manipulate pricing and discover the tools to protect yourself from predatory tactics.

The Bait

Dealers advertise incredibly low prices or "great deals" to lure customers into the showroom. These offers often include unrealistic financing terms or vehicles that don't actually exist.

The Switch

Once you're at the dealership, they claim the advertised car is "no longer available" and pressure you into a more expensive vehicle or add hidden fees that weren't disclosed upfront.

The Protection

Our tool analyzes real dealer pricing data to expose excessive fees and illegitimate charges, giving you the knowledge to negotiate fairly.
